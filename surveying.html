Surveying & Mapping Services

Premium Service Since 2015 4.9/5 Rating

We provide expert surveying and mapping services including cadastral, topographic, engineering, drone mapping, LiDAR, GPR, laser scanning, and bathymetric & hydrographic surveys.

Our Surveying Capabilities

Our surveying and mapping solutions cover the full spectrum of geospatial needs: cadastral surveys for land registration, topographic and engineering surveys for infrastructure design, and drone mapping for high-resolution aerial data.

We utilize cutting-edge technologies including GNSS, LiDAR, GPR, laser scanning, and hydrographic equipment for bathymetric mapping, enabling us to deliver accurate data for planning, development, and decision-making.

01

Client Engagement & Site Assessment

We begin by understanding your project needs through consultations and preliminary site visits to assess terrain, boundaries, or infrastructure requirements.

02

Survey Design & Equipment Mobilizatio

We design the appropriate survey methodology—be it cadastral, topographic, hydrographic, or LiDAR—and deploy field teams with GNSS, drones, total stations, or sonar tools as needed.

03

Field Data Collection & Ground Truthing

We collect spatial, elevation, or imagery data with precision instruments. Ground control points and reference markers are established where needed for accuracy validation.

04

Field Deliverables & Plan Implementation

We execute field tasks such as beaconing, demarcation, plan implementation (e.g. zoning layouts), land subdivision, and physical setting out of infrastructure alignments or plot boundaries.

04

Submission & Handover

Final deliverables—including hardcopy maps, digital files (DWG, SHP, PDF), and certified reports—are submitted. We also offer assistance with approvals or registration if required
