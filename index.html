Surveying & Geospatial Services

Cadastral, topographic, and engineering surveys, drone mapping, LiDAR, GPR, laser scanning, and bathymetric & hydrographic surveys.

GIS Solutions

Spatial data integration, mapping, and analytics; custom web & mobile GIS applications; smart city dashboards, public health, and security GIS.

Remote Sensing

UAV surveys, satellite imagery acquisition, terrain analysis, and LiDAR interpretation for land, urban, or environmental monitoring.

Civil Engineering & Infrastructure

Road and utility infrastructure planning, ESIA and RAP studies, site supervision, safety audits, and long-term infrastructure master plans.

Utility Modernization

IoT integration for electric and water systems, digital twin infrastructure modeling, asset tracking, and predictive maintenance systems.
